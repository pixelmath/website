10% Royalty on Content Contribution for PixelMath

March 31st, 2020

We are a team of designers, educators and Math enthusiasts.

We are designing a Maths Practice App - PixelMath - for students Grade 3 to 12. PixelMath employs principles of Game Design, Learning Sciences and Positive Psychology to make practicing maths fun and engaging.

To help us make creative and innovative content for our app, we are crowdsourcing our content creation. Contributors will be offered 10% royalty for their contributions.

The initial response for our app from the first 50 test users has been overwhelming. At present, we are working on incorporating feedback from our users and making the app better. We plan to launch the app by May 1st, 2020
