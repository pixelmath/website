GAME DESIGN

Uses elements from game design like - goals, adaptivity, balance and feedback mechanism to make maths practice fun and engaging.

POSITIVE PSYCHOLOGY

Incorporates elements of autonomy, competence and relatedness from positive psychology to keep players motivated and induce "flow" during maths practice.

LEARNING SCIENCES

Helps students calibrate their efforts during individual practice sessions by boosting their metacognitive abilities.

MACHINE LEARNING

Employs machine learning algorithm to learn from the players' previous responses and presents problems to student at the right challenging level.

COLLABORATION

Incentivizes increased collaboration and consistent practice through innovative leaderboards and user activity feeds.

DATA ANALYSIS

Identifies areas where the student is struggling and prepares advanced diagnostic reports for parents / teachers.
